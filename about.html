About the site

Planning help built around real cruise decisions.

Lido Deck Insider is an independent guide for Carnival cruisers. The site is designed to answer practical questions: which ship fits, where a cabin may create problems, what to do first, how to manage a large-ship layout, and where timing matters most.

Our editorial standard

We separate confirmed ship facts from planning analysis. Venue names, included-versus-paid designations, and ship features are checked against current official sources when a guide is reviewed. Strategic recommendations are clearly presented as independent advice, not official instructions.

What we do not do

We do not pretend that a generic article outranks current information from the captain, crew, cruise line, port, or destination authorities. Schedules, procedures, pricing, menus, and policies change. Readers should verify time-sensitive details for their exact sailing.

Independence

Lido Deck Insider is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Carnival Corporation or Carnival Cruise Line. Carnival names, ship names, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Corrections

Accuracy matters. When a factual ship detail changes, the guide should be updated and its review date advanced. A public contact channel will be added before commercial launch.
