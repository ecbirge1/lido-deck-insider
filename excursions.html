The return-to-ship test

Before booking independently, answer five questions.

  1. How far is the activity from the pier?Use realistic traffic and tender assumptions, not map-app optimism.
  2. Who controls the return transportation?A shared shuttle with fixed departures creates different risk than a private driver.
  3. What happens if weather changes?Know cancellation, rescheduling and refund terms before the port day.
  4. What is the group’s actual pace?Mobility, children, heat and bathroom stops all affect the schedule.
  5. How large is the return margin?We prefer a deliberate buffer rather than treating all-aboard as the target arrival time.
