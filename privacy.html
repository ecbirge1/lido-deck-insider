Privacy policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Newsletter and checklist form

The current static site does not transmit newsletter submissions to Lido Deck Insider or an email provider. When a visitor submits the checklist form, the email address is stored only in that visitor's browser using local storage so the site can reveal the printable checklist. It is not sent to the site operator. Clearing browser storage removes that locally stored value.

When a secure email-delivery provider is connected, the form and this policy will be updated before addresses are transmitted or used for marketing.

Interactive planning tools

The Ship Matcher, Cabin Risk Checker, Cruise Cost Calculator, and worksheet preview run in the visitor's browser. The answers and calculations are not transmitted to Lido Deck Insider by the current static implementation.

Payments and digital delivery

The paid toolkit checkout is not yet connected. Before sales begin, the site will identify the payment and digital-delivery providers involved. Those providers may process payment, contact, device, and transaction information under their own privacy policies.

Server and platform data

GitHub Pages and a visitor's browser, network provider, or security software may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and request logs as part of delivering the site. Those systems operate under their own terms and policies.

Cookies and analytics

This version of the site does not intentionally set advertising or analytics cookies. Browser local storage is used only for the checklist form behavior described above. If analytics, advertising, affiliate tracking, or similar services are added later, appropriate notices and controls will be implemented before deployment.

External links

Links to cruise lines, ports, merchants, payment providers, or other websites lead to independently operated services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Changes

This policy may be revised as the site adds email delivery, checkout, analytics, advertising, or affiliate services. The effective date will be updated when material changes are published.
