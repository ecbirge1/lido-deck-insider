Editorial and affiliate disclosure

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Independent publication

Lido Deck Insider is an independent cruise-planning publication. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Carnival Corporation, Carnival Cruise Line, or any other cruise line. Company names, ship names, logos, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Editorial approach

Ship facts and time-sensitive details are checked against current primary sources when a guide is reviewed. Recommendations, route strategies, and planning opinions are the site’s independent analysis. Official instructions from the cruise line, captain, crew, ports, and destination authorities always take priority.

Affiliate links

The initial release does not contain paid affiliate links. The site may later earn a commission when readers purchase through clearly disclosed links, at no additional cost to the reader. Commercial relationships will not guarantee favorable coverage or prevent critical analysis.

Sponsored content

Any future sponsored content will be labeled prominently. Sponsorship will not be disguised as independent editorial coverage.

Prices and availability

Prices, menus, policies, availability, itineraries, and schedules can change. Readers should confirm current terms directly with the relevant provider before booking or purchasing.
