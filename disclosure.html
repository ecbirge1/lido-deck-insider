Transparency

Editorial, product, and affiliate disclosure

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Independent publication

Lido Deck Insider is an independent cruise-planning publication. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Carnival Corporation, Carnival Cruise Line, or any other cruise line. Company names, ship names, logos, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Editorial approach

Ship facts and time-sensitive details are checked against current primary sources when a guide is reviewed. Recommendations, ratings, route strategies, tool results, and planning opinions are Lido Deck Insider's independent analysis. Official instructions from the cruise line, captain, crew, ports, and destination authorities always take priority.

Our own digital products

Lido Deck Insider has created the Complete Cruise Planning Toolkit, an eight-page printable PDF planned for sale at $19. The product is complete, but checkout and automatic private delivery are not yet connected. Any purchase button shown before that connection is activated does not process a payment.

Revenue from Lido Deck Insider products may support research, hosting, content updates, and development of additional planning tools. Product ownership does not change the site's obligation to describe limitations, pricing, and intended use accurately.

Affiliate links

The current site does not contain active paid affiliate links. The site may later earn a commission when readers purchase travel services or products through clearly disclosed affiliate links, at no additional cost to the reader.

Affiliate relationships will not guarantee favorable coverage, alter ship ratings, or prevent critical analysis. Pages containing affiliate links will include a clear disclosure near the relevant recommendation or commercial section.

Sponsored content

Any future sponsored content will be labeled prominently. Sponsorship will not be disguised as independent editorial coverage, and sponsors will not receive control over factual conclusions or planning recommendations.

Tool results and estimates

The Ship Matcher, Cabin Risk Checker, and Cruise Cost Calculator provide planning guidance based on the information entered and the site's editorial scoring model. Their results are estimates, not guarantees, professional advice, live inventory, or official cruise-line information.

Prices, availability, and policy changes

Prices, menus, policies, age restrictions, cabin benefits, venue availability, itineraries, and schedules can change. Readers should confirm current terms directly with the relevant cruise line, travel provider, merchant, or payment provider before booking or purchasing.

Corrections

Material factual errors should be corrected when identified. Guide review dates are intended to show when ship information received its latest substantive check.
